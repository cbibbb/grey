Main Causes of Food Insecurity

Low Income

Many families struggle because wages do not keep up with rising costs.

High Food Prices

Inflation has made groceries more expensive for many households.

Transportation Issues

Some families cannot easily travel to grocery stores.

Food Deserts

Some neighborhoods lack stores that provide fresh food.
