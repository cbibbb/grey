Resources for Food Assistance

Learn about organizations and programs helping fight food insecurity in Middle Tennessee.

Local Organizations

Second Harvest Food Bank of Middle Tennessee

Provides food assistance to families across Middle Tennessee through food banks, meal programs, and community partnerships.

Visit Website

MTSU Student Food Pantry

Helps college students experiencing food insecurity by providing free groceries and support services.

Government Programs

SNAP Benefits

Helps low-income families purchase groceries through monthly food assistance.

School Lunch Programs

Free or reduced lunch programs help children receive meals during the school day.

WIC Program

Supports women, infants, and children with access to healthy foods.

Community Solutions

Local churches, nonprofits, community gardens, and food drives also help reduce food insecurity. Many neighborhoods host donation events and volunteer opportunities to support families in need.

  • Meal donation programs
